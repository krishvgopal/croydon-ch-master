How you can pay

Telephone

Our automated payment line is available 24 hours a day on 0845 3000 614.  Please select option 04 for sundry debt invoices.  You will need your invoice number - «debtorref»«inv_no».  Please note there is a surcharge of 1.65% if you pay by credit card.  There is no surcharge for debit cards.

 

internet

Online payments can be accepted at www.croydon.gov.uk.   Select the “Pay for it” option on the front page and “Housing benefit overpayment” from the list of payment types.  From the Online Payments screen, select “Housing benefit overpayment recovery”.  Please enter your invoice number «debtorref»«inv_no» in the reference number box.  Please note there is a surcharge of 1.65% if you pay by credit card.  There is no surcharge for debit cards.

 

Paypoint

Payments by cash can be made at any outlet displaying the “Paypoint” logo.  You will need your invoice which has a printed bar code unique to you.

 

Post office

You can pay by debit card, cheque or cash at any Post Office although a fee of £2.50 may be charged.  You will need your invoice which has a printed bar code unique to you.

 

At the bank

Payments can be made at any bank, although a fee may be charged.  Please make cheques payable to “Croydon Council” and write your housing benefit reference and invoice number on the reverse of the cheque.  To use this method you will need the giro slip attached to the invoice.

 

BACS or electronic banking

Payments can be transferred via Nat West Bank, account name: LB Croydon, account number: 01000624, sort code: 57-10-70.  The payment should be referenced in the following format 23/«debtorref»«inv_no» (excluding any zeros at the beginning of the number) so your payment can be applied to your invoice.

 

post

Cheques can also be sent to: Croydon Council, Cash & Control, Bernard Weatherill House, 8 Mint Walk, Croydon, CR9 1BQ.  Please enclose your giro slip with your payment.  Cheques should be made payable to “Croydon Council” and include your housing benefit claim reference and invoice number written on the reverse.  Do not send cash through the post.  If you require a receipt, please include the invoice or a letter stating “Please send a receipt”.

 
 

 

instalments

To make an arrangement to pay your invoice by instalments, please phone 020 8667 8246 (for arrangements only) Monday to Friday – between 8am - 6pm or send an email with your contact details to: CDREC1@croydon.gov.uk.

 

help & advice about your housing benefit claim or the overpayment

If you need any general advice regarding this overpayment or your benefit claim, you can phone the contact centre on 020 8726 7000.  The opening times are Monday to Friday 8am to 6pm.  E mail: croyhben@croydon.gov.uk  Fax: 020 8667 8440.

 

If you need some more information about your benefits you can sign up to a customer account today by visiting https://myaccount.croydon.gov.uk.  This will give you instant access to your benefit account where you can view your entitlement, find out how we have worked out your benefit and see details of your payments at any time.
